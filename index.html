About Muhammad Wasswa Mwanje

Muhammad Wasswa Mwanje is a dedicated public servant with over 15 years of experience in community development and leadership. Born and raised in our district, he understands the challenges our people face and is committed to creating sustainable solutions.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To transform our district into a model of sustainable development, where every citizen has access to quality services, economic opportunities, and a voice in governance.

Mission

To provide visionary leadership that prioritizes education, healthcare, infrastructure, and youth empowerment while fostering transparency, accountability, and community participation in decision-making.

Key Promises

Education for All

Improve school infrastructure and provide scholarships for needy students.

Better Healthcare

Upgrade health centers and ensure adequate drug supplies.

Road Infrastructure

Prioritize road construction and maintenance across the district.

Job Creation

Support youth entrepreneurship and attract investors to create jobs.

Clean Water

Ensure every village has access to clean and safe water sources.

Transparent Governance

Implement open budgeting and regular community accountability forums.

Development Plans

Education Revolution

  • Construct 10 new primary schools in underserved areas
  • Provide scholarships for 500 bright but needy students annually
  • Teacher training programs to improve quality of education
  • Establish vocational training centers in each sub-county

Healthcare Improvement

  • Upgrade 5 health center IIs to health center IIIs
  • Ensure consistent drug supply in all health facilities
  • Mobile clinic services for remote communities
  • Maternal health programs to reduce maternal mortality

Infrastructure Development

  • Construct 100km of new roads in the first 2 years
  • Regular maintenance of existing road network
  • Install solar street lights in trading centers
  • Improve market infrastructure across the district

Youth Empowerment

  • Establish youth innovation hubs in each division
  • Provide startup capital for 200 youth businesses annually
  • Skills training programs in high-demand sectors
  • Create district youth sports league to nurture talent

Agricultural Development

  • Provide improved seeds and farming inputs to farmers
  • Establish agricultural extension services in all sub-counties
  • Support value addition through processing facilities
  • Create market linkages for farmers' produce

Women Empowerment

  • Microfinance programs targeting women entrepreneurs
  • Maternal health and childcare support programs
  • Adult literacy programs for rural women
  • Support women's groups with income-generating projects
